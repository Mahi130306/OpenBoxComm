Welcome to Open Box! We're a tech community dedicated to helping developers grow and connect.

## What is Open Box?

Open Box is a community of developers and creators who are passionate about technology and innovation. We provide multiple Discord servers, resources, and support for developers at all levels.

## Why Join?

- **Learn from experts**: Connect with experienced developers
- **Build together**: Collaborate on projects and ideas
- **Network**: Meet like-minded developers
- **Resources**: Access guides, documentation, and tools

## Getting Started

1. Join our main Discord server
2. Introduce yourself in the community channel
3. Explore resources and find your niche
4. Start building!

## Community Values

We believe in:
- **Collaboration** over competition
- **Knowledge sharing** and mentorship
- **Inclusivity** and respect
- **Innovation** and experimentation
